Healthy Diet Guidelines

Limit or Avoid

Cut back on processed foods, sugary drinks, and excessive fats.

These foods are typically high in sugar, unhealthy fats, salt, and additives, which can lead to chronic diseases like obesity, diabetes, heart disease, and inflammation.

Focus on Variety and Balance

Include different food groups in every meal for a balanced diet.

According to the Healthy Eating Food Pyramid, we have to eat a variety of foods among all food groups as well as within each group in order to get different nutrients and meet our daily needs.

Drink Sufficient Water

Stay hydrated by drinking 6-8 cups of water daily.

Staying hydrated helps regulate body temperature, aids digestion and supports healthy skin.

Mind Your Portions

Be aware of portion sizes to maintain a healthy weight.

Eating the right amount helps your body get the nutrients it needs without excess calories.

Eat Fruit and vegetables

Fruit and veggies provide vital nutrients, antioxidants, fiber, and energy while helping prevent diseases and maintaining overall well-being.

Healthy Eating Plate

The "Quarter-Quarter-Half" rule is a simple and effective way to maintain a balanced and nutritious diet:

  • ¼ Plate: Whole grains like brown rice, oats, or whole-wheat pasta
  • ¼ Plate: Lean proteins such as chicken, tofu, fish, or legumes
  • ½ Plate: A colorful mix of fruits and vegetables

This visual approach helps promote portion control and encourages a healthy relationship with food.
