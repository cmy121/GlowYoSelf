Types of Expressive Arts Therapy

Art Therapy

Involves drawing, painting, or sculpting to explore emotions and improve mental well-being.

Music Therapy

Uses listening to or creating music to reduce stress, improve memory, and express feelings.

Dance/Movement Therapy

Uses body movement to support emotional, cognitive, and physical integration of the individual.

Drama Therapy

Incorporates storytelling, role-play, and performance to express emotions and resolve conflicts.

Writing Therapy

Encourages journaling or poetry to reflect, release emotions, and promote self-understanding.
