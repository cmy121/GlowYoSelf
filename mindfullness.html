Daily Mindfulness Practices

Mindful Breathing

Focus on your breath. Inhale slowly through your nose for 4 counts, hold for 2, then exhale through your mouth for 6 counts. Repeat for 5 minutes.

Body Scan

Sit or lie comfortably. Close your eyes and slowly scan your body from head to toe, noticing any sensations without judgment.

Mindful Walking

Walk slowly and deliberately. Notice the sensation of your feet touching the ground, the movement of your body, and your surroundings.
