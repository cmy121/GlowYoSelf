Roasted Shrimp, Tomatoes, and Spinach

Quick sheet pan meal with caramelized wedges of onion and fennel, shrimp and juicy tomatoes.

Active Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 3 hrs 10 mins

Servings: 10 servings

Excuses don't burn calories

Ingredients:


  • 2 small red onions, cut into 1/2-inch-thick wedges
  • 1 small bulb fennel, cut into 1/4-inch-thick wedges
  • 1/2 tsp. coriander seeds, cracked
  • 1 Tbsp. olive oil
  • Kosher salt and pepper
  • 10 large peeled and deveined shrimp
  • 1/4 tsp. ground sumac
  • 12 oz. small cherry or grape tomatoes.
  • 1 small lemon, halved
  • 3 cups baby spinach

Directions:

  1. -Heat oven to 450Â°F. On rimmed baking sheet, toss onions, fennel, and coriander with oil and a pinch each of salt and pepper. Roast 15 minutes.
  2. -Season shrimp with sumac and 1/8 teaspoon each salt and pepper. Toss tomatoes into fennel mixture.
  3. -Then nestle in shrimp and lemon halves and roast until shrimp are opaque throughout, 5 to 7 minutes more.
  4. -Transfer half each of lemon, roasted vegetables, and shrimp to airtight container for lunch the next day.
  5. -Transfer remaining shrimp and lemon to plate. Scatter spinach over remaining vegetables and roast until barely wilted, 1 to 2 minutes.
  6. -Serve with roasted shrimp and lemon.
Nutrition Profile:

Sesame-Free, Nut-Free, Dairy-Free, Soy-Free, Egg-Free
