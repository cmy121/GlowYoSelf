High-Protein Marry Me Chicken

This flavorful, creamy chicken dish is packed with tender meat and a rich sauce, and it's easy to customize with protein-boosting ingredients.

Cal/Serv: 448

Total Time: 40 mins

Servings: 4 servings

Big on protein, bigger on taste.

Ingredients:


  • 3/4 cup chicken bone broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup whole milk cottage cheese
  • 2 Tbsp. chopped sun-dried tomatoes
  • 2 Tbsp. finely grated Parmesan
  • 1 tsp. tomato paste
  • Kosher salt and black pepper
  • 2 Tbsp. all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. Italian seasoning
  • 2 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp. unsalted butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1/4 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves, torn, plus more for serving

Directions:

  1. -In a blender, puree broth, cream, cottage cheese, tomatoes, Parmesan, and tomato paste until smooth; season with salt and black pepper.
  2. -Pat chicken dry; season all over with salt and black pepper. Sprinkle with flour and Italian seasoning, pressing with your fingers to adhere.
  3. -In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat oil. Working in batches if needed, cook chicken, turning halfway through, until browned on both sides.
  4. -Transfer to a plate and let rest 5 minutes.
  5. -In same skillet over medium-low heat, melt butter. Add garlic and red pepper flakes and cook, stirring and scraping browned bits from bottom of skillet, until fragrant.
  6. -Return chicken and any accumulated juices to skillet. Pour sauce over. Add basil and cook, spooning sauce over chicken, until sauce has slightly thickened, 5 to 7 minutes; season with salt and black pepper.
  7. -Divide chicken among plates. Top with more Parmesan and basil.
Nutrition Profile:

Low in carbs, High-protein, Refined sugar-free, Egg-Free

Why we should consider eating high protein food?

		High-protein foods help build and repair muscles, support weight management, and boost metabolism.
		They also promote bone health, stabilize blood sugar, and supports immune function.
