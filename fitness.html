Why Live Healthy?

Living a healthy life means that you are needed to be able maintaining physical, mental, and emotional well-being. It involves eating nutritious food, staying active, getting enough rest, and managing stress.


Benefits of Having a Healthy Life

A healthy lifestyle provide varies advantages for the overall quality of life.

  • Prevents Disease
  • Save Money
  • Extends Life Expectancy
  • Enhanced Vitality
  • Achieving Quality Sleep
  • Improves Skin Quality
  • Cognitive Enhancement
  • Reduce the Chance of Having Mental Health Issue

Exercise Information

Cardio Exercise

Why? : Increased stamina, heart and lung fitness, and reduced fatigue.

The Amount of Time : 30 minutes a day, 3 to 5 times per week



Strength Training

Why? : Improve your ability to do everyday activities, protect your joints from injury and have better balance for reducing your risk of falls.

The Amount of Time : 1 hour a day, 2 to 3 times per week



Yoga

Why? : Improve balance and flexibility.

The Amount of Time : 30 minutes a day, 2 to 3 times per week



Jogging

Why? : Strengthen muscles and bones.

The Amount of Time : 15 minutes a day, 5 times per week



Light Walking

Why? : Reduce postprandial glucose concentrations and diastolic BP.

The Amount of Time : 15 minutes a day, Everday



Low-Impact Strength Training

Why? : Strengthening muscles without incurring a lots of wear and tear on your joints.

The Amount of Time : 1 hour a day, 2 times per week



Aqua Aerobics

Why? : Water's resistance creates a challenging workout that helps burn calories.

The Amount of Time : 1 hour a day, 3 times per week
